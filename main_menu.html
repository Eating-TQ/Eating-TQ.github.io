Main Menu

Sweet Sakura provide both main menu, and season limited menu. Sweet Sakura provide both dine in and take out. Consumers also can order online, and pick up in the store.

Dessert provited for dine in are freshly made, and they are preferable for dine-in. Dine-in featured also include some ice dessert, and consumers can choose and build their own plate.
